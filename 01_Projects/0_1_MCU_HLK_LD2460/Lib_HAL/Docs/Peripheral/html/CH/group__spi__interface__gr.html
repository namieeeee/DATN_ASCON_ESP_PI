Description

SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) implements synchronous serial bus for data exchange. In MCU applications, interfaces are often used to connect board sub-level (PCB) peripheral components. SPI can configure as Master(SCLK and SEL are output) or Slave (SCLK and SEL are input)。


Feature

  1. Supports Motorola SPI, National Semiconductor MICRO-WIRE.
  2. Supports master mode or slave mode.
  3. 5-bit to 16-bit frame.
  4. Maximum SPI0 speed of 48 Mbps (master) or 32 Mbps (slave).
  5. Maximum SPI1/2 speed of 36 Mbps (master) or 32 Mbps (slave)
  6. Programmable serial data transfer format (MSB or LSB first).
  7. The start phase of data sampling location selection is 1st-phase or 2nd-phase controlled register.
  8. Supports internally or externally controlled serial bit clock.
  9. Supports internally or externally controlled frame/sync.
  10. Programmable frame/sync.
  11. Programmable serial bit clock polarity, phase, and frequency.
  12. Programmable threshold interrupt of transmit/receive FIFO.
  13. Supports DMA transfer.


配置设置 (sn34f78x_hal_conf.h)

  1. 如果需要使用 SPI 外设，需要打开 configUSE_SPI_PERIPHERAL
  2. 如果要使用 Blocking 传输，需要打开 configUSE_SPI_BLOCKING
  3. 如果要使用 IT 传输，需要打开 configUSE_SPI_NONBLOCKING_IT
  4. 如果要使用 DMA 传输，需要打开 configUSE_SPI_NONBLOCKING_DMA
  5. 如果要使用 Callback回调函数，需要打开 configUSE_SPI_REGISTER_CALLBACKS


信号线

信号 名字 说明
SEL Slave Selection (Polarity Programmable) 选择Slave；信号可以是SPI外设的一部分或者使用某个GPIO引脚引入。
SCLK Serial Clock 来自Master的串行时钟输出；数据伴随着时钟进行传输。
MOSI Master Out,  Slave In Master的MOSI输出连接至Slave的MOSI输入。
MISO Master In,  Slave Out Master的MISO输入连接至Slave的MISO输出。
